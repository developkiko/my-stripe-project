Multi-unit system

Using the multi-unit system

World-Implant introduces a multi-unit system that expands the range of options for doctors and their patients.

The use of multi-unit abutments allows fixing the prosthetic structure using screw fixation, without the use of cement, which in turn makes it possible to remove the prosthetic structure if necessary.

Multi-units are used for prostheses supported by two or more screw-retained implants. Available in various versions: straight multi-units and angled multi-units 17 and 30 degrees with gingival heights from 1.5 to 4.5 mm.

Corner multi-units have a single orthopedic platform with a cone with a hexagon.

The recommended tightening torque for fixing the multi-unit abutment is 35 Ncm, for the prosthetic screw 15 Ncm.
