About prosthetic components

The combination of innovations and technologies, research and testing made it possible to create a product that meets modern trends in implantology and is not inferior in quality to the best world analogues.

For a flawless, first-class result, World-Implant offer a wide range of prosthetic components that form an integral part of the surgical protocol.

Prosthetic components have a high manufacturing precision and parameters that bring the result of your work to perfection.

The prosthetics are made of Grade 5 titanium and are completed with two screws. Also in our assortment there is a unique narrow shaper, the use of which allows you to get the maximum amount of space for plastics of soft tissues, in the process of fouling of which they acquire the required volume and shape. Thanks to the use of this component, it is possible to achieve the natural contour of the gums and their volume, and the patient will receive the desired beautiful and healthy smile as a result.

Perfection is in the details!
