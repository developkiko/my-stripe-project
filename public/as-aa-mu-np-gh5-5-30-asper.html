World-Implant expands the scope of treatment opportunities and offers its customers a multiunit system - with maximum versatility and mobility.

The use of multiunit abutments allows you to fix the orthopedic structure with screw fixation without cement mixture, which in turn allows you to remove the orthopedic structure if necessary. This conditionally removable design from World-Implant is designed to be easy to install for doctors and an ideal aesthetic result for patients.

The range of the company initially includes implant sizes that can cover most clinical cases. The designers and clinicians have carried out a detailed analysis of the required shapes, diameters and lengths, as a result, we can assert that using Bauer’s Implants, you can easily achieve the most predictable positive results in the rehabilitation of patients.

Guarantee

Bauer’s Implants provides a lifetime warranty for its own implants. We can say with confidence that we are 100% confident in our products thanks to high-tech production, multi-stage quality control and our own clinical research.
